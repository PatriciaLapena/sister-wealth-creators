Questions We Hear Often

Can you guarantee I will make money? +
No. No ethical business can guarantee income. What we can do is help you create a thoughtful business foundation, practical assets, and smart systems designed to support visibility, action, and income-building opportunities. Your results will depend on your effort, your market, and your consistency.
Do I need to know what business I want to start? +
No — that is part of the process. We use your Soul File and Dream Life / Income Vision to generate business ideas that fit your skills, experience, energy, and goals. Many of our clients come in with no idea and leave with a direction they are genuinely excited about.
Do I need to be tech-savvy? +
No. The process is guided. The technology supports the business build, but you do not need to become a tech expert. We design systems that work for you — not systems you have to learn to operate.
What is a Soul File? +
Your Soul File is the foundation of everything we build. It captures your experience, strengths, talents, dreams, values, goals, and lifestyle vision — so the business we design is built around who you actually are, not a generic idea of who a business owner should be.
What is the Dream Life / Income Vision process? +
It is our guided process that helps you define your dream lifestyle down to the penny — calculating exactly how much you need to earn, broken down into yearly, monthly, weekly, daily, and hourly income targets. These become the planning goals for designing your business. They are planning goals, not guarantees.
Is Post Stream or monthly support required? +
No. All monthly support options are completely optional. You can choose to do your own social media and ongoing updates, or subscribe to the level of support that helps you stay consistent. There is no pressure and no obligation.
How long does the build take? +
Most builds are delivered within 2–3 weeks after the intake process is complete, depending on scope and how quickly you complete your questionnaires. We will keep you informed throughout.
Should I start with the Blueprint or go straight to the Business Build? +
If you already have a strong sense of what kind of business you want to build, go straight to the Business Build. If you want to explore your options first and get 10 personalized business ideas before committing, start with the $97 Next Chapter Business Blueprint — and apply your $97 toward the full build if you decide to upgrade within 14 days.
Is there a payment plan? +
Yes. The Next Chapter Business Build is available as 3 payments of $525 each. The Business Enhancement Build is available as 2 payments of $525 each. Contact us at support@reclaimedvitality.ca to arrange your payment plan before purchasing.
